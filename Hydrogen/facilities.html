Facilities

Library

The library facility is available to all students of the school. All classes from class I onwards are provided with at least one library period per week.

Computer Center

There is well equipped and Hi-Tech computer lab. Computer education is provided from Class I onwards.

Laboratories

To enhance the learning ability and skills, the school has a number of laboratories, such as: Physics Lab, Chemistry Lab, Biology Lab, 3D Lab, Mathematics Lab.
